# Discovery 101: Uncovering Pain
Discovery is about shifting the conversation from our features to their business friction.
* **The 70/30 Rule:** The prospect should be talking 70% of the time.
* **Questioning Framework:** Start broad ("Tell me about your current workflow") then drill down ("How much revenue does that downtime cost you?").
* **Active Listening:** Summarize their pain back to them to ensure alignment before proposing a solution.
