# Executive Alignment
Strategic accounts require selling to C-suite goals, not technical features.
* **The Currency of Executives:** They care about risk, cost, and time-to-value.
* **The Meeting Prep:** Research the company's annual report and public strategic initiatives before the call.
* **The "So What?" Test:** If you mention a technical capability, immediately explain how it impacts their bottom line or quarterly objectives.
