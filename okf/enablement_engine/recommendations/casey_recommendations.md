### Recommended: Technical Discovery
- **AI Coach Insight:** Here is a 2-sentence coaching tip for a Solutions Architect on conducting effective technical discovery:

During technical discovery, focus on building rapport with stakeholders and asking open-ended questions to gather not just the "what" but also the underlying business needs and constraints that will drive your solutions, rather than simply documenting requirements. This approach enables you to uncover hidden dependencies and priorities that may not be immediately apparent, ensuring your solution truly addresses the customer's pain points and provides long-term value.

- **Playbook Details:** # Enterprise Technical Discovery
In enterprise accounts, you are auditing their technical environment for "Revenue Leakage."
* **System Map:** Inventory their current stack (e.g., AWS, Salesforce) and identify where data silos are causing friction.
* **The Deep Dive:** Look for "hidden" technical debt, such as manually updated spreadsheets or legacy CRM sync issues.
* **Validation:** Frame every technical finding in terms of business cost (e.g., "This integration gap is causing a 5% data decay rate in your pipeline.").
